Code of Conduct

The mission of the FC Bayern München Fan Club New York City is to connect FC Bayern fans from New York City and the tri-state area, and offer an environment, which allows both our members and guests to support our club and enjoy themselves.

I. All members are ambassadors of our Fan Club and FC Bayern München, and we ask you to behave in a cordial, respectful, and proud manner when you wear a Bayern jersey.

II. We share our home bar Smithfield Hall with fan clubs of other teams. We therefore ask you to be polite and respectful towards fans and members of other clubs, as well as the staff and other guests. We do not condone abusive, assertive, or condescending behavior towards anyone.

III. Because our fan club comprises members of different national, racial, and cultural backgrounds, we do not tolerate racist, sexist, or any form of behavior, whether in person or in an online- forum, that intentionally or unintentionally offends a person.

Members who are found in violation of the Code of Conduct may face a ban from the fan club Facebook group, our bar Smithfield Hall, or even the fan club itself.
