FC Bayern State of Mind

Formed in Feburary 2012, FCB NYC held its first official meeting in August 2012 for Bayern's first match of the 2012-2013 Bundesliga season.

Since then, the small group that congregated each week for matches has formed into a powerhouse of passionate fans that has allowed us to become a prominent fanclub in New York City's football scene.
